# Sample Attribution

## Tabla & Dholak Samples

Real tabla stroke recordings sourced from the **Naad** project by Oormi Creations.

- **Repository**: https://github.com/oormicreations/naad
- **License**: MIT License
- **Author**: Oormi Creations (oormicreations@gmail.com)

The samples include individual tabla bols (strokes): Dha, Dhin, Na, Ta, Tin, Tun, Ge, Ke, Ti, Ga, and variations thereof. Different sample variations are used for tabla and dholak instruments to give them distinct tonal character.
